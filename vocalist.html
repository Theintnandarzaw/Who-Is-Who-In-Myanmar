Htoo Eain Thin


Burmese singer-songwriter

Htoo Eain Thin was one of the most popular and respected Burmese singer-songwriters. He brought a new style of pop rock music to the Burmese music scene in mid-1980s, and was popular till his death in 2004.

View more about Htoo Eain Thin

Lay Phyu


Burmese vocalist

Lay Phyu is a Burmese rock vocalist/guitarist. He rose to fame with his debut album Gandarya Lamin.

View more about lay phyu

May Sweet


Burmese Singer

May Sweet is a Burmese singer and actress, and considered one of the most commercially successful female singers in the history of Burmese pop music.

View more about May Sweet

Sai Sai Kham Leng


Burmese Singer

Sai Sai Kham Leng is a well-known Burmese singer-songwriter, model, novelist, and actor of ethnic Shan descent. He is known for his hip hop music.

View more about Sai Sai

Ni Ni Khin Zaw


Burmese Singer

Ni Ni Khin Zaw is a Burmese pop singer. She won the 2008 season of Melody World, a televised singing competition. Ni Ni performed in the opening and closing ceremonies of the 2013 Southeast Asian Games in Naypyidaw.

View more about Ni Ni Khin Zaw

Phyu Phyu Kyaw Thein


Burmese Singer

Phyu Phyu Kyaw Thein is a prominent Burmese pop singer, known for her stage presence and costumes. She has been a UNICEF celebrity ambassador since 2008
